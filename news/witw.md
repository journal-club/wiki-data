Whisper in the Woods (abbreviated WITW), is a newsletter published by the [Journal Club](/orgs/journal-club), released twice every semester. The first issue was published in September 2017.  The most recent issue of the magazine (Issue 2.1) was published in August 2018, a freshman edition.
